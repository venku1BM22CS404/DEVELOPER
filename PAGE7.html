PROJECT IDEAS:

  • Internet of Things (IoT) based weather monitoring system: Develop a weather monitoring system that uses IoT sensors to collect data such as temperature, humidity, and atmospheric pressure, and then transmit this data wirelessly to a central server or cloud database.

  • Wireless power transfer system: Build a wireless power transfer system that uses resonant magnetic induction to transfer electrical energy wirelessly between two or more devices, without the need for physical connections.

  • Real-time traffic monitoring system: Develop a real-time traffic monitoring system that uses wireless sensor networks to collect data on traffic volume, speed, and congestion, and then transmit this data wirelessly to a central control center for analysis and management.

  • Smart energy management system: Create a smart energy management system that uses sensors and wireless communication to monitor and control energy consumption in homes, buildings, or industrial facilities, helping to optimize energy usage and reduce costs.

  • Satellite communication system: Design and build a satellite communication system that can transmit and receive data, voice, and video signals from remote locations, enabling communication and connectivity in areas where traditional terrestrial communication systems are not available or practical.3
